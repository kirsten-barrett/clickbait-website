Mrs. Puff

Everyone know and loves Mrs. Puff, the town’s driving instructor. She actually failed Spongebob in his driving test over 1,258,058 times. She speaks so highly of herself and teaches adult driving classes daily. Little did you know, she wouldn’t have a job if anyone found out that she failed her driving license test twice AND got pulled over for reckless driving.
