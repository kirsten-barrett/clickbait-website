Spongebob Squarepants Uncovered

Spongebob

Spongebob is not a natural sea sponge. Look at all the other sponges in bikini bottom - they’re all naturally shaped. Spongebob Squarepants is perfectly yellow and square; just look at his parents! It’s rumored that a sailor, driven mad by scurvy, took his only sponge on deck and slapped eyes and a mouth on it. He worked tirelessly to create a robot brain out of scrap metal but he never succeeded. He then threw his creation overboard with frustration. Little does he know his robot sponge grew a personality and bought some pineapple real estate in bikini bottom!
